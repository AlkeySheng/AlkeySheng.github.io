上了赌桌的狗

发表于 2020-12-02 更新于 2020-12-06 阅读次数：

Version 1.0

CET-4、CET-6 写作 & 翻译

Words

Common

  • think
    • cling to the perspective that
    • argue
    • claim
  • many
    • multitudes of
    • numerous
    • plentiful
  • people /we /us
    • offspring
    • youngsters on campus
    • experts and professors
  • very
    • extremely
    • extraordinarily
  • important
    • significant
    • crucial
  • 人称代词作主语的句子都可以写成被动

Logic

  • And
    • similarly
    • likewise
  • But
    • nevertheless
    • conversely
  • So
    • therefore
    • consequently
  • Then
    • in addition
    • besides

Sentence

  1. 主语从句

    • 可以写在任何一句话前面拉长句子
    • ==It seems beyond dispute that==
  2. 定语从句
    • which引导的非限制性定语从句可以修饰前面整句话，翻译成 “这”。
    • He is a lady killer,
    • ==which has provoked the public’s widespread concern.==
    • 写在任何一句话的后面，对前面内容补充说明。

  3. 万能状语

    • ==as every one can see it==
    1. ==with the rapid advance of science and technology==
    2. ==in our contemporary society==
    3. ==in the general routine of everyday living==
    4. 可以写在任何一句话的前面 | 主谓间 | 末尾
  4. 插入语
    • 可以写在任何一句话主谓之间
    • ==as a matter of fact==
    • 某段第一句主谓间
  5. 强调句型
    • ==It is … that …==
    • It was yesterday that i met a crazy dog in the street.
    • 所有句子都可以写成强调句型，可写在某一段段首。

Article

Argumentation

Paragraph One

  1. 引出主题

    Recently the issue of … , ==as a matter of fact== , has been in the limelight into focus.

  2. 观点

    I cling to the perspective that + … + ==with the rapid advance of science and technology==.

Paragraph Two

  1. 举例

    ==It is== abundant ==that== examples to prove the statement are.

  2. 我有一个 真诚又有才华的 朋友

    The most persuasive one is the case of my sincere and talented friend Tom.

  3. He …

  4. Nevertheless, …

  5. Therefore, …

Paragraph Three

  1. 总结

  2. Under no circumstances can we fail to pay attention to the seriousness of the fact that … ==in the general routine of everyday living==.

  3. 措施

    • ==个人==

      Awareness about doing(being happy facing difficulties), ==as everyone can see==, could be cultivated to make ourselves lead a pleasant and favorable life.

    • ==父母==

      ==It seems beyond dispute that== parents are supposed to spend more time educating their kids to be happy facing difficulties.

      eg. to put eggs in different baskets.

  4. 口号

    The more actively ==sb.== face the issue, the more
    happily they will lead their life.

Proverbs & epigrams

Paragraph One

  1. 引出主题

    Recently the issue of … , ==as a matter of fact== , has been in the limelight into focus.

  2. 解释

    1. The meaning of the saying seems that …
    2. Similarly, … + ==in the general routine of everyday living==.

Paragraph Two

我有一个 真诚又有才华的 朋友

Paragraph Three

总结 + 措施 + 口号

Pictures & charts

Paragraph One

  1. 引 无可争议的

    ==It seems beyond dispute that== in the vivid (chart /cartoon) …

  2. 描述

  3. 总结中心

    Simple as the cartoon looks, its meaning behind is really so far—reaching——

Paragraph Two

我有一个 真诚又有才华的 朋友

Paragraph Three

总结 + 措施 + 口号

Letter

Paragraph One

  1. 自我介绍

    I am a senior from the department of art at Xinhua university.

  2. 目的

    I am, ==as a matter of fact==, writing the letter in order to …

Paragraph Two

  1. , to begin with,
  2. In addition,
  3. Consequently,

Paragraph Three

  1. Words fail me when I desire to express my sincere gratitude to you for your kind consideration my … (application/ complaint/ recommend)
  2. I look forward to a favorable reply at your earliest convenience.

Translate

  • 某单词不会

    • 上位词
    • 同义词 | 近义词
    • ==解释==
    • 大词瞎胡写 小词没看见

  • 英汉差异

    • 汉语意合 英语形合

      • 句子结构
      • 逻辑关系词

    • 英语多被动 汉语多主动

      • 时态 — 语态 — 主干

      • 在过去几十年里，政府采取了各种措施防止灾害发生。

        Various measures, in the past decades, have been taken to prevent disasters.

      • 那时，黄色是专为皇帝使用的颜色，皇家宫殿全都漆成黄色，皇袍总是黄色的，而普通老百姓是禁止穿黄色衣服的。

        At that time, yellow ==was designed== to use for the emperor, the royal palace ==was painted== yellow and the imperial robe was always yellow too. However, the ordinary people ==were not allowed== to wear yellow clothes.

      • 最近，中国科学院(Chinese Academy of Sciences )出版了关于其最新科学发现与未来一年展望的年度系列报告。

        An annual report about its latest scientific findings {and the prospect of the next year} was recently published by Chinese Academy of Science.

    • 英语多长句 汉语多短句

      • 非谓语动词

        • 长江==流==(ing)经多种不同的生态系统，是诸多濒危物种的栖息地，==灌溉了==(ing)中国五分之一的土地。

          The Yangzi River, ==flowing== through different ecological systems, keeps the habitat of plentiful endangered animals, ==irrigating== one fifth of chinnese land.

        • 大熊猫是熊科中最罕见的成员，主要==生活==(ing)在中国西南部的森林里。

          Pandas are the rarest members, mainly living in the forest of Southeast in China.

      • 从句

        • 中国人自古以来就在中秋节庆祝丰收, 这与北美庆祝感恩节非常相似。

          Chinese have had the tradition to celebrated harvest in the Middle-Autumn, ==which== proves quite similar with Thanks Giving Day in North America.

        • 乌镇是浙江的一座古老水镇，坐落在京杭大运河畔。这是一处迷 人的地方，有许多古桥、中式旅店和餐馆。

        • Wuzhen, an ancient water town of Zhejiang province, is located near the Beijing-Hangzhou Grand Canal, which is really a charming place with many ancient bridges, Chinese style hotels and restaurants there.

      • 连词

        • 在过去一千年里， 乌镇的水系和生活方式并未经历多少变化, 是一座展现古文明的博物馆。
        • In the past thousand years, the water system and the life way of Wuzhen has not gone through many changes. ==Therefore==, it now is the museum to show anicent civilization.

  • 翻译步骤

    • 以意群为单位读句子，确定句子时态语态
    • 确定句子的主干、非主干部分作为定语或状语
    • 每一句话翻译完都观察是否加逻辑关系词
